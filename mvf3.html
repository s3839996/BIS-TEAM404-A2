MVF3 - Live Tracking and Built in GPS

Description

Once a user enters a car park, a built-in map will open inside the app and with the user's permission, the app will use the current location of the user to help them navigate throughout the car park and assist them in finding the pre booked car park. Once a user arrives at the correct car park, the app will automatically start the count-down to when the e-ticket will expire. And as usual the GPS can help find the nearest parking location based on the user's current location.


Lucid Textual Information

  • Will require use of Location services in order to accurately track user of the application and when they have reached their destination.

  • Once the user has booked their desired parking spot, a map will appear with a direct path to the located parking space. This will give directions to the parking spot with on screen prompts, while also giving a clear map for the user to follow and prepare for upcoming turns, lane changes, etc.

  • Once at the parking location, the countdown will replace the on screen map and slowly countdown, depending on how long the user has booked their parking space for.

  • Once they have finished with the parking space, they may tap a button to end their session. This will bring them to the payment screen in which they will pay for the amount of time they booked the parking space for.

  • Any extra time will be added on as +xx:xx, and the user will be charged extra in the final receipt. This will be calculated based on the extra time.


User Stories

"As a parent of a novice driver, I would like to ensure the safety of the vehicle and track the parked location".

'Quick Park' should be able with the permission of the user, navigate and track the vehicle to ensure that there are no issues with it. Parents with novice drivers will be especially worried about their child either parking in illegal spots or unable to find their vehicle. With the Live tracking and Built-in GPS, the security of the car can be guaranteed and the user can easily locate the car without much external effort.


"As a new car owner, I would like to ensure the safety of my car and keep track of its location."

Being a new car owner, individuals can have possible security risks and can be worried about their car being stolen or damaged. The use of the 'Quick Park' application which incorporates the live tracking and the built-in GPS will provide the owners of the vehicle a peace of mind, and they can ensure that their vehicle is safe and sound. They can monitor it regularly throughout the day and can be provided live updates of vehicle status. This is extremely handy is a user is required to move the car to another parking location so they are not required to remember the location, but can easily access the details just through some clicks of a button.
